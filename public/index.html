Our Solution: The SC-Horn

A smart, context-aware horn system for acoustic civility

🔊

Dual-Activation System

Short Tap: Civility Alert (60dB polite chime)

Long Press: Emergency Alert (110dB safe horn)

📍

Contextual Logic

GPS and time-based enforcement of "Quiet Zones" and "Quiet Hours," restricting emergency alerts during sensitive periods

📊

Data Logging

All emergency alerts logged with timestamp, location, and duration for city enforcement and driver feedback

🎯

Frequency Calibration

Tuned to car horn's fundamental frequency range (300–600 Hz) for accurate detection and replacement
