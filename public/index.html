BEARZ: Silencing Unnecessary Honking

A data-driven solution to urban noise pollution through honking tracking, accountability, and behavioral transformation.

The Noise Pollution Crisis

Why Honking is a Problem

Urban honking has become a major source of noise pollution, affecting millions of people's health and quality of life.

65% of drivers honk inappropriately in non-emergency situations
90-110 dB - Car horns exceed safe noise limits (safe limit: 70 dB)
418 times/year - Average honking frequency per driver
4.56 million vehicles registered in the UAE

Health & Environmental Impact

Sleep Disruption: Noise pollution reduces sleep quality and increases fatigue-related accidents.

Stress & Anxiety: Chronic exposure to honking increases cortisol levels and stress-related illnesses.

Cardiovascular Issues: Prolonged noise exposure contributes to hypertension and heart disease.

Behavioral Problems: Children exposed to noise pollution show reduced concentration and learning ability.

Environmental Degradation: Noise pollution disrupts wildlife and ecosystems in urban areas.

The BEARZ Solution

How BEARZ Works

BEARZ is a compact honking tracking device installed in vehicles during annual RTA registration. It uses advanced frequency sensors to detect and log honking incidents, creating accountability through a tariff system.

🔊 Frequency Detection

Accurately captures car horn frequencies (300-600 Hz) to track only the vehicle's own honks, not ambient noise.

📊 Data Logging

Records honking incidents with timestamps and duration for accurate accountability and tariff calculation.

💰 Tariff System

Drivers pay annual tariffs based on honking frequency, creating a financial incentive to reduce unnecessary honking.

🌍 RTA Integration

Seamlessly integrates with existing vehicle registration systems across all 7 UAE emirates.
